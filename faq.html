what is a vat refund?

a vat refund is provided to all non-eu tourists who visit the eu. all non-eu tourists are entitled to a vat refund. the vat in ireland is 23% so therefore you can recieve up to 23% back minus a small processing fee of 5%.

how is my tax free shopping different to other companies?
