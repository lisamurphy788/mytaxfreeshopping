faqs

how does this service work?

this is different to all other services. you are in charge of how much you get back. traditionally refund providers would work with retailers and offer tax free shopping through there tills. when this was happening the customer was getting a huge part of the refund taken away. with our service you only pay a very small processing fee.

what is a vat refund?

a vat refund is provided to all non-eu tourists who visit the eu. all non-eu tourists are entitled to a vat refund. the vat in ireland is 23% so therefore you can recieve up to 23% back minus a small processing fee of 5%.

how is my tax free shopping different to other companies?

with my tax free shopping you are only three steps away from your refund. you can shop in any store, from small local stores to global brands, and get your refund through our easy-to-use mobile app. all you need to do is upload your receipts and travel documents to the app! its as easy as that.
